**Sip, Relax, Restore**

**Unwind Organic Tea** is a **calming herbal blend** crafted to **help you slow down**, **reset,** and **restore balance** naturally. Made with **100% organic**, **ethically sourced ingredients**, this **caffeine-free** tea combines **lemon balm**, **peppermint,** **ginger root**, **licorice root**, and **bacopa leaves** to **support relaxation** and **mental clarity**. Whether you're **winding down after a long day** or creating a **mindful moment in your routine**, this **soothing** loose-leaf tea delivers a **smooth,** **refreshing taste** while **supporting overall well-being**.

**What's Inside**

- **Lemon Balm:** Supports relaxation by calming the nervous system which helps reduce stress and promote a peaceful state of mind
- **Peppermint Leaves:** Aids digestion and provides a cooling sensation that leaves you feeling refreshed and balanced
- **Ginger Root:** Helps stimulate circulation and digestion which supports overall wellness and comfort
- **Licorice Root:** Adds a smooth natural sweetness while soothing the throat and supporting respiratory health
- **Bacopa Leaves:** Supports cognitive function which may help improve focus and mental clarity
- **Aromatic Experience:** Releases calming herbal aromas during steeping which enhances relaxation and mindful moments
- **Refreshing Crisp Taste:** Delivers a refreshing yet mellow flavor that feels both uplifting and relaxing creating a smooth, well-balanced cup you’ll look forward to every time
